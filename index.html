Quadtree

Hit N, space, enter, or the down arrow to add a point with a random position to the quadtree.

The left panel will update to display the territory covered by the quadtree nodes. The right panel will show the quadtree's internal node tree. You can drag to pan around the panel and use the mouse wheel to zoom.

Click on a point or quad on the left to see corresponding tree node on the right. Likewise, click on a node on the right to see the corresponding point or quad on the left.

Here's what a quadtree is. The quadtree implementation used here is D3's quadtree (source). I put this together to make it clearer how the quadtree rearranges itself to accommodate points that are added to it. The source is here.
